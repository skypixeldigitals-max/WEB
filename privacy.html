Privacy policy

Last updated: July 2026

This privacy policy explains how Shakir Jamaldeen Photography ("we", "us") handles information collected through this website. By using the site or contacting us, you agree to the practices described here.

Information we collect

When you submit the contact form, we collect the details you choose to provide — your name, email address, phone number (optional), preferred shoot date (optional), subject and message. We do not collect payment information through this website.

How we use it

We use the information you send solely to respond to your enquiry, discuss a potential project, and provide a quotation or schedule. We do not sell or rent your personal information to anyone.

Third-party services

Contact form submissions are delivered by email through a third-party form service (FormSubmit). Your message is transmitted to our email inbox to allow us to reply. These providers process data only to deliver the message to us.

Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Any essential storage is limited to what is needed for the site to function.

Data retention

We keep enquiry emails for as long as needed to respond and maintain a record of our correspondence. You may ask us to delete your details at any time.

Your rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have shared with us. To do so, email shakir.jamaldeen@gmail.com.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact Shakir Jamaldeen at shakir.jamaldeen@gmail.com or +94 77 753 6266.
